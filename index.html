My Story

I grew up in northern Pakistan along the Afghan border & immigrated to Canada in my early teens. I started my career as a software engineer, graduating from UWaterloo, working on infrastructure like data, cloud, and DevOps. Over time, I realized I was drawn to the business side of things, which ensures what we build serves a purpose.

At 22, I built a profitable e-commerce business producing ergonomics products, scaling it to a cash-flowing venture. Later, I became a product manager in San Francisco, growing physical product portfolios and building analytics dashboards for inventory and pricing.

Now, I’m exploring the deeper layers of the tech stack, like infrastructure, and how it enables the development of transformative technologies like AI.
