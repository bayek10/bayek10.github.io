My Story

I grew up in northern Pakistan along the Afghan border & immigrated to Canada in my early teens. I started my career as a software engineer graduating from UWaterloo, working on the infrastructure side along data, cloud & DevOps. Over time, I realized I was drawn to the business side of things, which I view as the thing that ensures what we're building actually serves a purpose. That's exactly what was missing from my original work as a software engineer where my perspective of the world was equal to looking through a keyhole — building things at companies but having little idea how it connects to the world or has an impact or aligns with my life path (which I didn't know what that was either).

I decided to step away and pursue that curiosity further. Knowing nothing about business, I decided to build one completely by myself at the age of 22, producing ergonomics products like laptop stands & gel sitting cushions for those of us who sit at a desk all day. I managed to scale this into a profitable cash-flowing business that gave me freedoms of time & location I previously had no access to, which I used to explore living in other parts of the world (Dubai).

Not a tech business, I know. But 'business' doesn't discriminate. At the end of the day, it's just a collection of skills that applies to any type of business — strategy, customer insight, product development, market differentiation, customer psychology, financial management, personal accountability, delegation & self-growth.

As I was figuring out my business & operations skills, I started working as a product manager in San Francisco at an e-commerce holding company. I grew their portfolio of physical products & helped build an analytics dashboard on top to optimize & automate things like inventory forecasting, dynamic pricing & advertising.

Now? I have a renewed interest in the deeper layers of the tech stack, aka infra, and how it enables the development & distribution of key technology like AI. There are challenges in this part of the stack that I want to help solve, that I believe have a large impact on everyone.

I am currently exploring where I am best suited to contribute. If you'd like to speak, please shoot me a message on LinkedIn or X.
